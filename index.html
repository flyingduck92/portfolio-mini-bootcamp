Process

Structured, collaborative, and always a little bit playful — here's how I work.

Every great product starts with honest questions. I map user needs, business goals, and technical constraints to build a shared vision before a single pixel is designed or a line of code is written.

From rough wireframes to high-fidelity interactive prototypes, I ensure every interaction is intentional. Motion and micro-animation are baked in from the start — GSAP is a core part of my design language.

I ship in short sprints, gathering real feedback early. Code is clean, accessible, and tested. Performance budgets are respected. I blog about what I learn — because teaching is the best way to master a skill.

Launch is the beginning, not the end. I track analytics, run usability sessions, and keep a tight feedback loop to ensure the product keeps improving long after go-live.
